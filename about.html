Our Purpose

Weaving Potential into Performance

Who We Are: TeamWeave Consulting is a boutique organizational strategy firm. We combine the rigor of educational psychology with the agility of modern management consulting.

What We Do: We deconstruct chaos and reconstruct clarity. Through strategic workshops, culture interventions, and leadership retreats, we turn fragmented groups into unified teams.

Why We Do It: We believe that work should not be a place of friction. When a team is aligned, work becomes a place of flow, purpose, and profound impact.

Meet the Founder

Dr. Barnabas Amu

"Education is the tool to change the world, but Leadership is the hand that wields it."

Dr. Barnabas Amu is a visionary educator, organizational strategist, and the Executive Director of Abuja International Academy. With a background spanning educational leadership, project management, and corporate consulting, he brings a unique, multi-disciplinary lens to solving team challenges.

Known for his philosophy of "Excellence in the small things," Dr. Amu believes that true leadership is built on consistency, dependability, and a genuine care for the people you lead.

When he isn't facilitating strategy sessions, you can find him advocating for fitness, faith, and family values.
